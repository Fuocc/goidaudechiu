FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • Do you accept male customers?

    Of couse! Ý welcome both men and women to Ý Ơi Spa.

  • Do you have a couple/dual room?

    We do! Please save your spot at our 2nd location (Ý Ơi Spa – 31/26 Hoàng Hoa Thám) and Ý will reserve a lovely, private room for 2 people to experience our services.

  • How do I get to the spa?

    You can book a Taxi, Grab, or XanhSM to one of our locations at:
    📍 62/20 Lê Văn Huân, Tân Bình Ward, HCM City
    📍 31/26 Hoàng Hoa Thám, Tân Bình Ward, HCM City

    If there's any question, please don't hesitate to call Ý at (+84) 968241808 so we can instruct the local drivers.

    You can also drive, take a bus and walk to our spa.

    We have free car and scooter parking available on site.

  • When will you stop taking appointments?

    Our last booking time is at 8:00 PM daily; after 8:00 PM our practitioners will stop taking in new customers and we'll close after completing all remaining services.

    However, you can book your spot 24/7 outside of business hours ❤️.

  • Do you accept credit cards?

    No, unfortunately. We only accept cash and bank transfers, including payment app like MoMo.
